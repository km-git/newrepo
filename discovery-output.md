## 7. Cursor Pro Power User Tips (Cheap-First)

1. **Default to Auto or Haiku 4.5 — never frontier.** With 50-55 bets where most get cut in 7 days, premium models on experiments is burning money. Haiku handles schema, planning, and coding for 90% of MVPs. Save Fable/Opus 4.8/Sol for bets that already have paying customers.

2. **Cmd+K is your default tool, not chat.** Open the file, select code, Cmd+K with Haiku 4.5. Chat burns context tokens; Cmd+K doesn't. Target 80%+ of edits via Cmd+K. This alone cuts spend by 60-70%.
